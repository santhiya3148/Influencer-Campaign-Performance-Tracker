# 📊 Influencer Campaign Performance Tracker – Nykaa Marketing Campaign Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **Nykaa's marketing campaign performance** using campaign-level data to measure the effectiveness of different marketing channels, campaign types, customer segments, and audience engagement.

The dashboard helps marketing teams understand campaign performance, optimize marketing spend, improve customer acquisition strategies, and maximize Return on Investment (ROI).

This project was developed as part of the **AI & ML Internship Program – Task 06: Influencer Campaign Performance Tracker**.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Brands invest significant budgets across multiple marketing channels such as Influencer Marketing, Paid Ads, Social Media, Email Marketing, and SEO.

However, without proper analytics it becomes difficult to answer questions like:

- Which campaign type performs best?
- Which marketing channel generates maximum revenue?
- Which customer segment responds the most?
- Are marketing campaigns profitable?
- How can future campaigns be optimized?

This dashboard provides data-driven insights to answer these business questions and improve marketing decisions.

# 📈 KPI Definitions

### Total Campaigns
Total number of campaigns conducted.

### Total Revenue
Overall revenue generated from all campaigns.

### Total Marketing Spend
Total acquisition cost incurred.

### Total Impressions
Overall audience reach.

### Total Clicks
Total customer interactions.

### Total Conversions
Number of successful conversions.

### Average ROI
Average Return on Investment across campaigns.

### Average Engagement Score
Average engagement generated by campaigns.

### Click Through Rate (CTR)

```text
CTR = (Clicks / Impressions) × 100
```

### Conversion Rate

```text
Conversion Rate = (Conversions / Clicks) × 100
```

### Cost per Acquisition (CPA)

```text
CPA = Acquisition Cost / Conversions
```

---

# 💡 Key Findings

- Paid Ads and Influencer campaigns contribute significantly to revenue generation.
- Some low-cost campaigns produce higher ROI than expensive campaigns.
- Engagement score has a positive relationship with conversions.
- Certain customer segments consistently outperform others.
- Campaign performance varies across marketing channels, indicating the need for channel-specific strategies.

---

# ✅ Recommendations

- Increase investment in campaigns with consistently high ROI.
- Focus on high-performing customer segments for better conversions.
- Optimize or discontinue campaigns with low engagement and poor ROI.
- Allocate budgets based on historical performance instead of equal distribution.
- Continuously monitor campaign KPIs using the dashboard for data-driven decision making.
